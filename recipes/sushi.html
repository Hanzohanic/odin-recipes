Sushi Recipe

Some Sushi, To shush out the day!

A traditional Japanese dish of prepared vinegared rice

Portioned prepared vinegared rice topped or rolled with other ingredients....

Ingredients Used!

  • Prepared vinegared rice
  • Sugar & Salt
  • Seafood(raw)
  • Vegetables

Here are the steps!

  1. Preparing Your Sushi Rice
  2. Heat your vinegar, sugar, and salt together in a small saucepan
  3. Add your seasoning mixture to the cooked rice and let it cool
  4. Cut your filling ingredients into small pieces(vegetables)
  5. Place a sheet of nori on your rolling mat with the shiny side down
  6. Spread a thin layer of sushi rice onto the sheet of nori
  7. Make a shallow depression near the bottom of the rice with your finger
  8. Add roughly (57 g) of filling ingredients to the rice
  9. Roll the sushi up from the bottom using your mat as a guide
  10. Cut your roll into 1–2 in (2.5–5.1 cm) pieces
  11. Serve your roll with other toppings and condiments of your choosing
