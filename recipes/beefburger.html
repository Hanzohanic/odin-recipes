Beef Burger Recipe

Here is a nice yummy beef burger with alot of cheeeeese!

The classic burger is an all time BBQ favourite!

Double pettie with alot of cheeeeese!

Ingredients Used!

  • Â½ tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, peeled and finely chopped
  • 1 x 500g pack British Beef Steak Mince 15% fat
  • 1 tsp mixed dried herbs
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 4 slices mature Cheddar (mandatory!)
  • 2 white rolls
  • Lettuce Leaves
  • Tomato
  • BBq sauce

Here are the steps!

  1. Heat the olive oil in a frying pan, add the onion and cook for 5 minutes until softened and starting to turn golden. Set aside.
  2. In a bowl, combine the beef mince with the herbs and the egg. Season, add the onions and mix well. Using your hands, shape into 2 patties
  3. Cook the burgers on a preheated barbecue or griddle for 5-6 minutes on each side. While the second side is cooking, lay a slice of cheese on top to melt slightly
  4. Meanwhile, lightly toast the cut-sides of the buns on the barbecue. Fill with the lettuce, burgers and tomato slices. Serve with BBq sauce
